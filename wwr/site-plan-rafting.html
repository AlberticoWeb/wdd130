Overview

Our purpose is to offer excellent customer service, from the reservation to the river. Our team will guide you through the whitewater and make sure you have the best whitewater rafting experience possible. We want your experience to be as pleasant and come back to us again. Saldivar's Rafting Company has been the best choice in recent years from the reasonable low prices, the knowledgeable and attentive staff. Without a doubt, the experience has made the difference and after 10 years of taking more than 350,000 clients along the river, we have proven it. Our objective is also based on caring for the environment, so on each trip our guides raise awareness and involve our travelers, deeply appreciating the preservation of our rivers and natural ecosystems. As well as emphasizing the impact of human beings on the environment, so we educate our clients for each encounter with local cultures, animals, and plants.

For all those adventurous people who enjoy feeling the fresh water splashing on their faces, spend time together with family and friends while enjoying some of the most beautiful destinations in the world. Saldivar Rafting Company achieves all this without breaking the family harmony. We offer beginner, intermediate and advanced river rafting trips suitable for all ability levels. From family floats to high adrenaline whitewater! , as well as creating smiles and lasting memories through whitewater rafting and outdoor adventures. Since our main objective is to minimize the risks associated with adventure travel. Travel involves physical exertion and exposure to excessive heat, the possibility of cold, ,sun, wind, rain and snow. We have experience accommodating people with a wide range of physical disabilities and/or health conditions. However, individuals who have other physical limitations or ailments that interfere with nature encounters may endanger themselves, other guests,and guides. We recommend consulting your physician if you have any health or medical conditions that may affect your ability to participate in outdoor adventures.
